About Us

We are so excited to introduce to you the first annual Bossa Americana Festival in SF! Featuring local and visiting Brazilian or Brazilian-American artists and creators, Bossa Americana looks to help connect those in the Brazilian-American diaspora in SF to better build and unify our community! Bossa Americana is the place where community and creativity meet, featuring samba dance lessons and music from some of the great pop and bossa nova artists of all time!

Our Sponser

BrasArte

Central to BrasArte’s mission is cultural exchange between the USA and Brazil. An integral part of this is organizing tours and workshops with artists directly from Brazil, starting at its inception with the group Ile Aiye: Bahia’s first Bloco Afro, and Nicinha Raizes de Santo Amaro: one of the oldest surviving samba de roda groups in Brazil.
